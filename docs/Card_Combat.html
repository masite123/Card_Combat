Card_Combat

Version:
  • 1.0.1
Author:

Card_Combat.js is a module to play "card combats". Cards are drawn by the players from the deck to battle the opponents, until only one player is left. Play moves clock-wise through the 'summon'; 'switch'; and 'battle' phases, until all but one players are eliminated.
